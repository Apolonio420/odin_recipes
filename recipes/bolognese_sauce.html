Bolognese Sauce

Description

A rich and meaty Italian sauce that's perfect for pasta.

Ingredients

  • 500g minced beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a pot, saut√© onions and garlic until translucent.
  2. Add the minced beef and cook until browned.
  3. Add tomatoes, wine, and spices. Simmer for at least 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
