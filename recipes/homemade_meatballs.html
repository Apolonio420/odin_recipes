Homemade Meatballs

Description

Delicious and juicy meatballs, perfect for spaghetti or sub sandwiches.

Ingredients

  • 500g minced beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. In a bowl, combine all ingredients thoroughly.
  3. Shape into balls and place on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until fully cooked.
