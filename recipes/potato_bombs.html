Potato Bombs

Description

Crispy on the outside and fluffy on the inside, these potato bombs are a treat.

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped chives
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes until they're soft. Mash them.
  2. Add cheese, chives, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Shape into balls and deep fry until golden brown.
