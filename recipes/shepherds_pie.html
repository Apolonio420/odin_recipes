Shepherd's Pie

Description

A classic British dish, Shepherd's Pie combines lamb, vegetables, and mashed potatoes for a comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • 500g minced lamb
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup beef or lamb stock
  • 4 large potatoes, boiled and mashed

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a pan, sauté the onions, carrots, and garlic until tender.
  3. Add the minced lamb, cooking until browned.
  4. Add the stock and let simmer until thickened.
  5. Transfer the meat mixture to a baking dish and cover with the mashed potatoes.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the top is golden brown.
